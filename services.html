Ceremony Services
Officiant
Your Words. Your Way. Your Forever.

A wedding ceremony is the only moment in your day that actually matters legally and spiritually — and it deserves to feel completely, unmistakably like you. Zach has been officiating weddings in the Smoky Mountains long enough to know that the best ceremonies aren't the fanciest ones. They're the most personal ones.

Whether you want something short and sweet, deeply traditional, faith-based, or entirely your own invention — Zach works with you to craft a ceremony that captures who you are as a couple. He'll meet with you, listen to your story, and write something you'll want to read again years from now.

"I still get emotional at ceremonies. That's not a problem — that's a promise that this work still means something to me."

DJ & Sound Services
Music & DJ
The Soundtrack to Your Love Story

Music sets the tone for every single moment — from the second guests arrive to the last song of the night. Zach doesn't just show up with speakers and a playlist. He curates the entire sonic experience of your wedding day, reading the room, honoring your vision, and keeping the energy exactly where it needs to be at every turn.

Whether you want a romantic acoustic processional, a high-energy reception, or something entirely unexpected — Zach will work with you to build a playlist that feels like yours. He handles all sound equipment, transitions, and MC duties so you never have to worry about a thing.

And because he's already your officiant option too, when Zach handles both ceremony and reception music, the whole day flows as one seamless, intentional experience.

Floral Design · Bridgett Speegle
Florals
Natural Beauty, Thoughtfully Arranged

Bridgett approaches florals the way she approaches everything — with genuine care and a deep attention to what makes each couple unique. Her designs aren't pulled from a catalog. They're built from a real conversation about who you are, what you love, and how you want your wedding day to feel when you look back on the photos.

Drawing inspiration from the natural beauty of the Smoky Mountains — wildflowers, lush greenery, earthy textures, seasonal blooms — Bridgett creates arrangements that feel organic and alive, not stiff or overdone. Whether you're dreaming of something soft and romantic or lush and dramatic, she'll design florals that look like they were always meant to be there.

"There's a moment when a bride sees her florals for the first time. I live for that moment."
