How It All Began

We Know What It Feels Like
to Say "I Do."

Because we've said it — and meant it every day since.

We're Zach and Bridgett, and together we've built something that started with a simple belief: every couple deserves a wedding team that genuinely cares. Not just about the logistics — but about the meaning behind every moment.

After more than 27 years of marriage, we understand what it takes to build a life together. We know the weight a vow carries, the joy of a first dance, the way a well-placed flower can make a room feel sacred. That lived experience is what we bring to every wedding we're honored to be part of.

"We didn't start Vow Makers to be vendors. We started it because we wanted couples to have what we have — a team standing beside them that actually believes in their love story."

— Zach & Bridgett, Vow Makers

We're based right here in Sevier County, Tennessee, in the shadow of the Great Smoky Mountains — one of the most breathtaking places on earth to pledge your forever. And we've watched again and again as the mountains have a way of making everything feel more real, more sacred, more yours.

Vow Makers handles it all: the ceremony and vows, the music and DJ, the florals, and the planning. That means you have one trusted team, one consistent vision, and one less thing to worry about on the most important day of your life.

What We Believe

The Heart Behind
Everything We Do

We are marriage-centered, not trend-centered. These are the convictions that guide every wedding we're part of.

The Vow is Everything

Trends come and go. The promise you make to each other is what lasts. We keep that at the center of everything we plan and everything we say.

Beauty in Every Detail

From the floral arrangements to the playlist transitions, nothing is an afterthought. We believe the small things add up to the memory of a lifetime.

Your Day, Your Way

We don't have a formula. Every couple is different, and every wedding should feel like them — not like a template. We listen before we plan.

Calm Under Pressure

Wedding days don't always go to plan. We've seen it all, and we stay grounded so you can stay present. The best moments happen when you're not stressed about logistics.
